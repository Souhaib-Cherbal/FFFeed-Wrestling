Privacy Policy for Multi-Video Facebook Uploader

Last updated: [Current Date]

1. Introduction

This privacy policy outlines how the Multi-Video Facebook Uploader ("we", "our", or "the application") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our Facebook application to upload multiple videos.

2. Information We Collect

We collect and process the following information when you use our application:

  • Video files that you choose to upload
  • Video titles and descriptions you provide
  • Tags associated with your videos
  • Your Facebook Page ID
  • Access token provided by Facebook (temporarily stored for the duration of the upload process)

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information solely for the purpose of uploading your videos to Facebook as a multi-video post. Specifically, we use this information to:

  • Upload your videos to Facebook
  • Create a carousel post with your uploaded videos
  • Apply the titles, descriptions, and tags you provide to your Facebook post

4. Data Storage and Security

We do not store your videos, access tokens, or any other personal information beyond the duration of the upload process. All data is processed in memory and is discarded once the upload is complete or if an error occurs.

5. Sharing of Information

We do not share your information with any third parties. The only data transfer occurs between our application and Facebook's servers for the purpose of uploading your videos and creating posts.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Correct any inaccuracies in your personal information
  • Delete your personal information from our systems

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new privacy policy on this page.
