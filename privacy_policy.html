Privacy Policy for FFFeed Wrestling

Last updated: September 19, 2024

1. Introduction

Welcome to FFFeed Wrestling. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our app to upload wrestling videos to various social media platforms.

2. Information We Collect

When you use FFFeed Wrestling, we collect:

  • Wrestling videos you choose to upload
  • Titles and descriptions for your videos
  • Tags you add to your videos
  • Your social media account IDs (e.g., Facebook Page ID, X account ID)
  • Temporary access tokens provided by social media platforms

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information exclusively to:

  • Upload your wrestling videos to your chosen social media platforms
  • Create multi-video posts or carousels as specified by you
  • Apply your provided titles, descriptions, and tags to your posts

4. Data Storage and Security

FFFeed Wrestling does not store your videos, access tokens, or any personal information. All data is processed temporarily in the app's memory and is permanently deleted once the upload is complete or if an error occurs.

5. Information Sharing

We do not share your information with any third parties. The only data transfer occurs between FFFeed Wrestling and the servers of the social media platforms you're posting to (e.g., Facebook, X, Instagram, Threads, TikTok, YouTube).

6. Your Rights and Control

You have full control over your data. You can:

  • Choose which videos to upload
  • Decide which platforms to post to
  • Edit or delete your posts directly on the social media platforms

7. Changes to This Policy

If we update this Privacy Policy, we'll post the new version within the FFFeed Wrestling app and update the "Last updated" date.
